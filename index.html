About Me

My name is Kenten Roth, and I am currently an automotive technician. I am teaching myself how to code on my nights and weekends. I am looking to change careers to something that I find more interesting. I started to learn to code a long time ago, but over the last several months or so I have taken it much more serious and started to push hard towards finally changing careers. I have bounced between several different languages, but have settled on using JavaScript and React. My short term goal is to become a front-end developer, and down the road move towards becoming a full-stack developer.
